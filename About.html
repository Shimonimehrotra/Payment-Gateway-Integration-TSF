ABOUT US


Keeping in mind the need for having a dedicated fund with the primary objective of dealing with any kind of emergency or distress situation, like posed by the COVID-19 pandemic, and to provide relief to the affected, a public charitable trust under the name of ‘THE HOPE RELIEF FUND’ has been set up. The Hope Relief Fund has been registered as a Public Charitable Trust.
